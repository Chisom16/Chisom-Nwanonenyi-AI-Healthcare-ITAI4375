## About This Portfolio
This repository contains my work from ITAI 4375: Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare. Throughout this course, I explored how AI techniques such as machine learning, natural language processing (NLP), and reinforcement learning can be applied to real-world healthcare challenges.

Each lab and assignment in this portfolio demonstrates my understanding of key concepts and my ability to analyze how AI can be used in clinical and healthcare settings.

## Repository Structure
The repository is organized into the following sections:

- **Labs/** – Hands-on exercises and practice work  
- **Assignments/** – Detailed coursework exploring AI applications in healthcare  

Each folder contains:
- A README explaining the work, approach, and key takeaways  
- Supporting files (documents or code)  

## Projects Overview

### Assignments

- **Medical Terminology in Healthcare Research**  
  Analyzed key medical terms used in recent healthcare studies and their importance in clinical practice.

- **Healthcare Data Analysis**  
  Performed basic statistical analysis on patient data, including calculating averages and interpreting results.

- **Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS)**  
  Explored how AI-powered systems assist healthcare professionals in diagnosis and treatment planning.

- **Social Determinants of Health (SDOH)**  
  Examined how non-medical factors such as income, education, and environment impact health outcomes and healthcare decision-making.

- **Diagnostic Study Evaluation**  
  Evaluated a medical diagnostic test using key performance metrics such as sensitivity and specificity.

- **Prognostic Model Analysis**  
  Reviewed an AI-based model used to predict patient outcomes and analyzed its strengths and limitations.

- **AI in Medical Case Study**  
  Applied AI concepts to analyze a real-world cardiovascular case and identify potential risks and solutions.

- **Natural Language Processing in Healthcare**  
  Critiqued the use of NLP for analyzing unstructured medical records and discussed benefits and limitations.

- **Reinforcement Learning in Healthcare**  
  Designed a reinforcement learning scenario for optimizing medication dosing in critical care settings.

- **AI in Healthcare Glossary (Midterm Project)**  
  Developed a glossary of key AI terms and concepts used in healthcare, with definitions and real-world applications.
