# Assignment 4: Social Determinants of Health (SDOH)

## Problem
This assignment examined how non-medical factors influence health outcomes.

## Approach
I analyzed key social determinants such as income, education, and healthcare access, and their impact on health.

## Key Concepts
- Social determinants of health
- Health disparities
- Ethical considerations

## Results / Insights
Social and environmental factors play a major role in health outcomes and should be included in healthcare analysis.

## Reflection
This assignment helped me understand the importance of combining social data with AI to improve healthcare equity.
